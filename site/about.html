Who we are

We are a cross-functional group of battery scientists, data engineers, and reliability analysts focused on accelerating safe energy storage deployment. Our mission is to pair rigorous electrochemistry with modern data science so every battery pack leaves the factory performing at its best.

How to contribute

  1. Open an issue describing the improvement or dataset you would like to share.
  2. Fork the repository and create a feature branch named feature/your-topic.
  3. Follow the coding standards documented in CONTRIBUTING.md (coming soon).
  4. Submit a pull request with screenshots or sample outputs illustrating the change.

Need inspiration? Check the open issues for tasks tagged good first issue.

Contact

For partnership opportunities, data access requests, or operational support please reach out:

  • Email: hello@anomolous-cells.com
  • Slack: Join the #battery-anomaly channel in the internal workspace.
  • Newsletter: Subscribe to the quarterly update via our website.
