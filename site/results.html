Model performance snapshot

The ensemble achieves the following metrics on the most recent validation window (covering three production lots and 1,200 cells):

Precision @ 95% recall

81%

Alerts that progress to manual review remain highly actionable.

False discovery rate

19%

Below the 25% target agreed with manufacturing quality.

Time-to-detection

2.4 d

Median delay between impedance sweep and alert notification.

Alert review workflow

Alerts are exported as a CSV digest and mirrored to the manufacturing execution system (MES). Quality engineers triage findings using the following playbook:

  1. Confirm the alerting detector and review SHAP summaries for top contributing features.
  2. Cross-check with rate-test data and visualise Nyquist plots for the impacted cell.
  3. Decide whether to scrap the unit, rerun the measurement, or escalate for design feedback.

Communicating impact

Dashboards in reports/ aggregate weekly statistics that quantify financial and operational impact. Highlight the following when briefing stakeholders:

  • Scrap avoidance: Number of packs prevented from shipment thanks to early anomaly detection.
  • Process drift: Lots flagged for follow-up due to impedance centroid movement.
  • Model health: Drift scores and retraining cadence to maintain trust in automated screening.
